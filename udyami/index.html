Our Events.

Panjab University's National Entrepreneurship Summit; a 3-day entrepreneurial extravaganza 'UDYAMI 2017 - Think | Plan | Pitch' is scheduled from 24-26th February 2017 at Panjab University main campus. At Udyami, we aim at fostering the startup ecosystem of this developing nation by engaging with the country's youth through a plethora of events that include workshops, competitions, panel discussions, intern fair and networking sessions. With an expected turnaround of entrepreneurs, researchers, investors and students numbering over a thousand, connecting with talent, passion and vision has never been easier.

About.

Udyami - Entrepreneurial Crossroads is Panjab University's National Entrepreneurship Summit. Udyami aims to bring together entrepreneurs, students, industrialists and investors to create an ambience for new ideas and startups. Summit will include competitions designed to test and enhance the entrepreneurial skills, workshops and Panel Discussions covering all the major fields of entrepreneurship.

Competitions.

Innovation is important and competition makes you constantly innovate. They push you out of your comfort zone because of the quest to serve better. Udyami 2016 presents a wide range of competitions that will test your business acumen within a fixed time contraint.

Panel Discussions.

Udyami 2016 makes sure that the event remains as versatile as possible by including speaker and eminent guests from different domains and conducting panel discussions over them. Topics vary from Travel to Healthcare.

Workshops.

Going beyond the horizons of textbooks and making something innovative is certainly a measure to improve an individual in practical terms. Udyami 2016 provides such practices that make an individual more prone towards learning and getting better with each session.

Networking.

Networking is the perfect way to meet other entrepreneurs and make connections that could prove to be essential in the future. Date with the ones you want to move ahead further in your venture. With start-ups and investors around, one has the opportunity to build up his own network.

Who Are We.

In today's changing economic scenario college education isn't a guarantee of success. World needs a new vision which only creative and innovative entrepreneurs can provide. The entrepreneurship skills are rarely taught in college and what's better than to start early and we at EDC provide this learning opportunity.

Our Vision.

We believe that entrepreneurship can unlock any country's potential. It will not only push them to the forefront but also help raise the quality of life in the country. With this belief we at Entrepreneurship Development Cell, UIET target to help in the development of an entrepreneurial ecosystem.

Our Approach.

We try to develop this ecosystem by enabling efficient interaction between students, working professionals, aspiring and existing entrepreneurs, mentors, angel investors, venture capital firms and corporates through initiatives like interactive sessions, competitions and summits.

Our Goal.

To act as an institutional mechanism for providing various services including information, mentorship and funds to budding student entrepreneurs and to foster better linkages between organizations engaged in promoting Small & Medium Enterprises (SMEs) including NGOs and other Voluntary Organisations.

Our Mission.

Entrepreneurship Development Cell, UIET aims to create a sustainable and diverse entrepreneurial ecosystem where the young and the elderly come together to take entrepreneurship a step ahead in India.
